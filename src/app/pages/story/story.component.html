Vision

We want to get to the core of one’s medical issues instead of treating individual symptoms.

Mission

We want to solve and treat our patient’s medical ailments whether they be physical or mental. Our primary advantage is that we offer comprehensive medical care. Most primary care physicians refer out for many of the services we offer. Our core services include primary care, addiction treatment, geriatrics, weight loss, minor surgeries, XRAY, osteopathic manipulative treatment, medical spa, counseling services and blood testing on site.

Core Values

  • Respect - High regard for the opinions of one's self and others; we treat patients, families and co-workers with dignity.

  • Integrity - We practice medicine with honesty and confidentiality for all patients.

  • Accountability - We follow through on all commitments that we make to you and your health.

  • Compassionate Care - We demonstrate our care by truly listening to and addressing all of your concerns. We promise to treat you and your family with empathy.
