Vision

We want to get to the core of one’s medical issues instead of treating individual symptoms.

Mission

We want to solve and treat our patient’s medical ailments whether they be physical or mental. Our primary advantage is that we offer comprehensive medical care. High quality and comprehensive primary care is hard to find. Most primary care physicians refer out for many of the services we offer. We offer primary care, addiction treatment, geriatrics, weight loss, minor surgeries, XRAY, OMM, medical spa services, counseling services and blood testing on site.

Core Values

  • Respect - High regard for opinions of self and others; treat patients, families and co-workers with dignity

  • Integrity - Honesty and confidentiality; pursue service excellence

  • Accountability - Follow through on all commitments; every interaction is "every patient, every time"

  • Compassionate Care - Demonstrate caring by listening, expressing concern, and empathy for patients, families and co-workers
